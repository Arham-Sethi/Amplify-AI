7 Techniques That Amplify Every Prompt

Each prompt is automatically enhanced using research-backed prompt engineering methods.

Role Assignment

Assigns an expert persona so the AI responds with domain-specific depth and authority.

"You are a registered dietitian with 15 years of experience..."

Specificity Injection

Replaces vague words with concrete parameters, constraints, and measurable targets.

"lose weight" → "1-2 lbs/week for a 185lb sedentary male"

Chain-of-Thought

Instructs the AI to reason step-by-step before answering, dramatically improving accuracy.

"First calculate X, then design Y based on that result."

Output Format

Specifies the exact structure — tables, bullet points, JSON — so the output is immediately usable.

"Present as a table: Day | Meal | Calories | Protein"

Task Decomposition

Breaks complex requests into numbered sub-tasks so nothing gets missed or forgotten.

"Structure: 1) Summary, 2) KPIs, 3) Analysis, 4) Actions"

Negative Constraints

Tells the AI what to avoid — eliminating generic filler, hallucinations, and irrelevant tangents.

"No supplements, juice cleanses, or extreme diets."

Evaluation Criteria

Sets quality standards so the AI knows how its output will be judged and self-corrects.

"Evaluate on: accuracy, feasibility, cost efficiency."
