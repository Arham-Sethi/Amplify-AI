Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email address — to communicate waitlist updates and product announcements
  • Name (optional) — to personalize your experience

We also automatically collect basic analytics data (page views, referral source) using privacy-focused analytics that do not use cookies or track individuals.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your waitlist position and send your access code
  • To send product launch updates and early access invitations
  • To improve our website and user experience

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) for data storage with row-level security policies. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

Each service has its own privacy policy and maintains industry-standard security practices.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The only data stored in your browser is your session token (if you sign up for the waitlist), which is used solely to keep you logged in.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@amplifyai.com.
